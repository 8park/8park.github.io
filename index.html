My Path: From Engineer to Educator

My interest in education grew naturally from my experiences as an engineer and leader. Each step of my journey revealed a new piece of the puzzle.

Chapter 1: The Foundation in Precision

Prototyping Technician

My journey started with a fascination for turning ideas into reality. In the demanding automotive R&D world, I learned the non-negotiable value of precision. This purely technical role taught me that getting the details right is the foundation of any successful project.

Chapter 2: The Addition of Leadership

Artillery Maintenance Team Leader

Leading a 4-technician maintenance team taught me that technical skill alone isn't enough. I had to not only solve problems but also train my team to do the same. This was my first real taste of teaching – the challenge of transferring complex knowledge clearly and effectively under pressure.

Chapter 3: The Spark of Education

University Lab Technician & Instructor

This role was a turning point. While managing a lab with over 50 machines and 99% uptime, I also had the opportunity to teach. I noticed that everyone learned differently, and I began developing unique ways to explain it. This is where my passion for empowering others through education was born.

Chapter 4: The Confirmation

AI Researcher & Teaching Assistant at UTRGV

At UTRGV, I put my budding educational philosophy to the test. I applied my AI knowledge to a real-world USDA research project while teaching programming concepts to over 300 undergraduate students. Seeing them grasp complex ideas confirmed that my practical, hands-on approach worked. This is where my dream for education truly took shape.

View the Research Project →

Chapter 5: The Synthesis

Founder & Developer of FinitUp

To truly understand how technology creates business value, I decided to build a solution myself. FinitUp is my ongoing project to build an AI-powered stock analysis platform, directly applying the principles from my A-grade MBA courses in Finance and Economics. This is my 'Learning by Building' philosophy in action, merging my entire skillset to create tangible value.

Visit Finitup.com →

Chapter 6: The Current Mission

Founder of the UTRGV AI Club

Now, I'm channeling all these experiences into a single mission: making technology accessible. The AI Club is the culmination of my journey. It's a community built on the belief that anyone can learn and innovate with the right guidance and a hands-on approach, a principle I discovered through my own path from engineer to educator.

My Guiding Philosophy

Learning by Building

My philosophy isn't a theory; it's the result of my journey. I believe in learning by doing, making complex ideas simple through real-world application, and empowering others with the confidence to build their own solutions. This practical, hands-on approach guides everything I do.
