My Personal Story

My path has been driven by a constant desire to learn, lead, and contribute.

My journey began in South Korea, driven by a passion for leadership and technology that I cultivated through military and engineering roles. A desire for growth brought me to the U.S., where I now merge my expertise in Computer Science, AI, and business to create new value and empower others.

My Path: From Engineer to Educator

My interest in education was not planned; it grew organically from my experiences as an engineer and leader. Each step of my journey revealed a new piece of the puzzle.

Chapter 1: The Foundation in Precision

Prototyping Technician

My journey started with a fascination for turning ideas into reality. In the demanding automotive R&D world, I learned the non-negotiable value of precision. This purely technical role taught me that getting the details right is the foundation of any successful project.

Chapter 2: The Addition of Leadership

Artillery Maintenance Team Leader

Leading a maintenance team taught me that technical skill alone isn't enough. I had to not only solve problems but also train my team to do the same. This was my first real taste of teaching – the challenge of transferring complex knowledge clearly and effectively under pressure.

Chapter 3: The Spark of Education

University Lab Technician & Instructor

This role was a turning point. While helping students with their projects, I noticed that everyone learned differently. The challenge wasn't just knowing the material, but finding unique ways to explain it. I began to realize that I enjoyed the process of demystifying technology for others, and my interest in education was born.

Chapter 4: The Confirmation

AI Researcher & Teaching Assistant at UTRGV

At UTRGV, I finally put my budding educational philosophy to the test. I applied my advanced AI knowledge to solve a real-world research problem for the USDA, while simultaneously using my new teaching methods to help undergraduate students succeed. Seeing them grasp complex ideas confirmed that my practical, hands-on approach worked. This is where my dream for education truly took shape.

Chapter 5: The Current Mission

Founder of the UTRGV AI Club

Now, I'm channeling all these experiences into a single mission: making technology accessible. The AI Club is the culmination of my journey. It's a community built on the belief that anyone can learn and innovate with the right guidance and a hands-on approach, a principle I discovered through my own path from engineer to educator.

My Guiding Philosophy

Learning by Building

My philosophy isn't a theory; it's the result of my journey. I believe in learning by doing, making complex ideas simple through real-world application, and empowering others with the confidence to build their own solutions. This practical, hands-on approach guides everything I do.
